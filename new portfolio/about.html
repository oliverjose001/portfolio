About Me

My name is Jose Oliver, and I am from Chenbagaramanputhoor in Kanyakumari District. I completed my schooling at Government Higher Secondary School, Aralvoimozhi, and later pursued my Bachelor of Science in Mathematics at S.T. Hindu College, Nagercoil. My academic background has helped me build strong problem-solving and analytical skills, which I now apply in my journey as a developer.

After graduation, I discovered my passion for technology and decided to build my career in Frontend Development. I started learning on my own and dedicated time every day to understand the basics as well as the practical applications of modern web technologies. Through consistent practice and self-learning, I have gained skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and GitHub, which allow me to create structured web pages, style them with clean designs, add interactivity, and manage my projects effectively.

Although I am a fresher with no formal job experience, I am eager to take on opportunities where I can apply what I have learned, gain real-world exposure, and continue growing as a developer. My goal is to improve my technical expertise, collaborate with other professionals, and contribute to meaningful projects while developing myself into a skilled frontend developer.
